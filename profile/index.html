Profiles showcase your level, streak history, and prestige collection. This static lounge edition keeps profile data available on request while we migrate to a lighter experience.

Profile contents

Request your profile snapshot

  1. Email support@uberspinners.com from your registered address.
  2. Include “Profile Snapshot” in the subject line.

Updating your details

To change your display name or avatar, contact support with the requested update. Frame assignments follow the rules on the Prestige Frames page and cannot be manually edited.

Account privacy

Profiles are private by default. Leaderboard entries show display names only. To remove yourself from public leaderboards, email privacy@uberspinners.com.
