Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Display name, email address, password hash.
  • Game activity: Game history, progression, virtual currency balances.
  • Device information: Browser type, IP address, device identifiers.
  • Usage data: Interactions with features, session metadata.

2. How we use information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the lounge experience.
  • Track progression, streaks, and virtual currency balances.
  • Improve features and develop new content.
  • Communicate updates, surveys, and responsible play notices.
  • Prevent fraud, secure accounts, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers that operate our platform, comply with legal processes, or display limited public information (for example, leaderboard display names).

4. Data security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect personal information. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate analytics, remember preferences, and secure sessions. You may adjust your browser or visit the Cookie Settings page to manage preferences.

6. Your rights

You may:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Object to processing or ask for data portability.

Visit the Data Rights page for request instructions.

7. Children’s privacy

The lounge is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us for removal.

8. Data retention

We retain personal information while your account remains active and for a reasonable period afterwards to meet legal obligations. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymise applicable data.

9. Policy changes

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-lounge notice.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, email privacy@uberspinners.com.
