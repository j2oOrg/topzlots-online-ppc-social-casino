Prestige Frames

Frames are decorative keepsakes that wrap your profile portrait and leaderboard card. They celebrate consistency, community spirit, and milestone streaks-never spending.

Collection overview

Twelve core frames rotate through the lounge each season. Unlocking is straightforward: level up, complete daily streaks, or finish seasonal quests. Frames are purely cosmetic and cannot be traded or sold.

Bronze Metal

Starter frame awarded at account creation. Warm copper tones with etched suits.

Silver Metal

Reach level 10 to unlock this brushed chrome finish with subtle card glyphs.

Golden Ornate

Level 18 reward featuring filigree detailing and ambient glow animations.

Platinum Metal

Complete three consecutive seasons with positive community reputation.

Crystal Diamond

Claim ten daily bonuses in a row. Faceted crystal flourishes celebrate streak dedication.

Ruby Gem

Earned by finishing the “Firelight Festival” event quests. Radiant ruby shards pulse gently.

Sapphire Gem

Granted after hosting three community lounge nights or submitting featured feedback.

Emerald Gem

Unlocked through the “Verdant Voyage” mini-season. Highlights eco-inspired motifs.

Legendary Epic

Top three leaderboard finishers receive this electrified trim for the following season.

Mythic Cosmic

Reserved for community ambassadors recognised by moderation. Nebula hues orbit the border.

Floral Nature

Earn seasonal nature tokens during spring events to blossom this frame.

Neon Cyber

Participate in the “Synthwave Spectacle” weekend. Animated circuit patterns pulse with XP gains.

Frame etiquette

  • Frames are cosmetic only and do not alter odds or gameplay.
  • One frame can be active at a time; switch anytime from your profile dashboard.
  • Community guidelines apply-frames should not be used to impersonate staff.
  • Lost access? Email support with your display name and we will restore past unlocks.

Showcase tips

Mix frames with seasonal profile banners, update your bio with your current streak, and share screenshots in the lounge forums. We regularly spotlight creative combinations in our newsletter.
