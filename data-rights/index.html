Your Data Rights

Uberspinners respects privacy obligations under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). Use this page to understand and exercise your rights.

Rights for EEA/UK guests (GDPR)

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, along with details about how we process it.

Rectification

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information on your account.

Erasure

Request deletion of your data when it is no longer needed or when you withdraw consent.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

Restriction & Objection

Limit certain processing activities or object to use of your data, including for direct marketing.

Automated decisions

We do not run automated decision-making that produces legal or significant effects without human review.

Rights for California residents (CPRA)

Know & access

Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected in the preceding 12 months.

Deletion

Ask us to delete personal information, subject to limited exceptions (security, legal obligations, fraud prevention).

Correction

Request that inaccurate personal information about you be corrected.

Opt-out

Uberspinners does not sell or share personal data for cross-context behavioural advertising.

Limit use of sensitive data

We only process limited account identifiers necessary to provide the lounge-no biometric or precise geolocation data is collected.

Non-discrimination

We will never deny services, charge different prices, or provide lesser quality if you exercise your rights.

How to submit a request

  • Email privacy@uberspinners.com from the address associated with your lounge pass.
  • Include the right you wish to exercise (access, deletion, correction, etc.) and any relevant details.
  • We may ask for additional verification to protect your account. Verified requests receive a response within 30 days (45 days for complex cases).

Prefer not to email? Use the contact page and select “Data Rights” in your message.

Supervisory authorities

If you are dissatisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. EU residents can locate their authority at edpb.europa.eu. California residents may contact the California Privacy Protection Agency (cppa.ca.gov).

Contact our privacy team

Data Protection Officer enquiries: privacy@uberspinners.com. Please allow up to 30 days for a formal response after verification.

This notice was last updated October 27, 2025. We will update this page whenever privacy regulations or our practices change.
