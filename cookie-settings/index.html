Cookie Settings

This static version of Uberspinners no longer stores cookie preferences in the browser. Use the steps below to control optional cookies manually.

Always-on cookies

Essential cookies keep the site secure and functional (for example, session management and load balancing). They are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled.

Disable optional cookies

  1. Open your browser’s cookie controls (see the guides in our Cookie Policy).

  3. Set preferences to block third-party or non-essential cookies going forward.

Because this site runs without client-side scripts, analytics and advertising cookies are now inactive by default. Blocking them ensures they remain disabled.
