Accessibility Statement

Uberspinners is committed to providing an inclusive experience for all visitors. We continually review our lounge to align with WCAG 2.1 Level AA expectations and invite feedback at any time.

Our commitment

We design every update with accessibility in mind. This static, script-free edition of the lounge improves compatibility with assistive technologies and reduces cognitive load for guests who prefer predictable layouts.

We continue to audit content against Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and welcome community input whenever barriers remain.

Current accessibility features

Keyboard navigation

All interactive elements can be reached using the keyboard. Use Tab/Shift+Tab to move, Enter or Space to activate, and skip links to jump straight to main content.

Screen reader support

Semantic HTML, landmarks, descriptive headings, and alternative text support screen readers such as JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver.

Visual design

  • High contrast palette exceeding WCAG AA colour ratios.
  • Consistent navigation and layout throughout the site.
  • Visible focus indicators for links and buttons.
  • Text that remains legible when users zoom up to 200%.

Media alternatives

Imagery includes contextual alternative text. Decorative graphics are marked as such to avoid distraction for assistive devices.

Known limitations

  • The legacy interactive dashboard is no longer available in this static build. Some historical references may mention disabled features.
  • Embedded third-party game providers may include interfaces outside our direct control. We share feedback with partners continuously.
  • Older event archives may contain PDFs that require additional accessibility review. Contact us if you need accessible versions.

Feedback & support

Encountering a barrier? Email accessibility@uberspinners.com. Please include “Accessibility” in the subject line along with:

  • The page or feature you were using.
  • Your browser and assistive technology (if applicable).
  • A brief description of what happened and any suggestions.

We aim to acknowledge accessibility requests within five business days and resolve issues as quickly as possible.

Technologies we rely on

This site uses HTML5 and CSS3.
