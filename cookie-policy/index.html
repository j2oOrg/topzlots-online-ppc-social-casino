Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 2025

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how you interact with the lounge.

IAB TCF 2.2 compliance

Our consent banner aligns with the IAB Transparency & Consent Framework (TCF) v2.2. Your preferences are passed to participating vendors to respect your privacy choices across advertising platforms.

Types of cookies we use

1. Necessary cookies

Purpose: Essential for the website to function (authentication, security, session management).

Duration: Session-based or up to 12 months.

2. Analytics cookies

Purpose: Measure how visitors use the lounge (page views, time on page, navigation paths).

Examples: Google Analytics, Mixpanel.

Duration: Up to 24 months.

3. Advertising cookies

Purpose: Deliver relevant marketing messages and measure campaign effectiveness.

Partners: Google Ads, Meta Pixel, trusted ad networks.

Duration: Up to 13 months.

Third-party services

These providers may set cookies on our behalf:

  • Google Analytics: Usage analytics and reporting.
  • Google Ads: Advertising and remarketing.
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel: Social media advertising measurement.
  • Supabase: Authentication and backend services.

Managing your preferences

Control cookies by:

  • Visiting the Cookie Settings page to adjust optional categories.
  • Using your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Opting out of interest-based advertising via industry tools (for example, aboutads.info).

Blocking certain cookies may limit access to some lounge features.

Browser controls

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Updates to this policy

We may revise this policy as our use of cookies evolves. Any updates appear on this page with a new revision date.
