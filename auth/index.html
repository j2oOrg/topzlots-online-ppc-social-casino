Create Your Lounge Pass

Uberspinners is now delivered as a lightweight, script-free experience. To open a free lounge pass, follow the guided steps below and we will activate your account manually within one business day.

What you receive

  • 1,000 welcome chips deposited to your account once activation is complete
  • Access to the Daily Bonus streak tracker and prestige frames history
  • Personalised leaderboard card with your chosen display name

How to request access

  1. Prepare your preferred display name (3–15 characters, letters and numbers only).
  2. Include the country or region you are playing from so we can tailor responsible play resources.
  3. Email support@uberspinners.com with subject line “Lounge Pass Request”.
  4. We will reply with a secure activation link and temporary password.

We process new accounts manually to ensure age verification (18+) and platform compliance. There is no automatic verification or payment required.

Keeping your account secure

After your first sign-in, choose a unique password that you do not reuse elsewhere. Accounts are personal, non-transferable, and cannot be sold or traded. If you misplace your credentials, contact support for a reset link.

We never ask for payment information or identification documents. If you receive any request claiming to be from Uberspinners that mentions payment, please forward it to us immediately.

Need to deactivate?

You can close your lounge pass at any time by emailing support@uberspinners.com. Once closed, all virtual currency and progression data will be permanently removed.
